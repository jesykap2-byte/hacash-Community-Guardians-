# Hacash Community Guardians Manifesto

## Introduction

Hacash Community Guardians (HCGD) was created to recognize and encourage constructive participation within the Hacash ecosystem.

Healthy decentralized communities depend on more than technology alone. They require educators, moderators, contributors, and volunteers who help maintain an environment where knowledge can be shared and newcomers can participate confidently.

## Mission

Our mission is to acknowledge individuals who contribute positively to the Hacash community through:

* User education
* Technical guidance
* Community moderation
* Knowledge sharing
* Ecosystem advocacy
* Constructive discussion

## Core Principles

### Community First

The long-term health of the ecosystem takes precedence over individual interests.

### Education and Accessibility

Knowledge should be accessible to all participants regardless of experience level.

### Respectful Participation

Constructive discussion and mutual respect strengthen community resilience.

### Transparency

Community initiatives should operate with clear communication and documented processes.

### Sustainability

Recognition systems should encourage meaningful and lasting contributions rather than short-term activity.

## Commitment

HCGD serves as a community recognition token designed to celebrate participation and contribution within the Hacash ecosystem.

The project does not represent ownership rights, equity interests, or guarantees of financial outcomes.
