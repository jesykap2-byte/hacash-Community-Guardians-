# Hacash Community Guardians Roadmap

## Phase 1 — Launch

Objectives:

* Deploy HCGD token
* Publish project documentation
* Establish community channels
* Define contribution standards

Deliverables:

* Token launch completed
* Public documentation available
* Community onboarding resources published

---

## Phase 2 — Community Recognition

Objectives:

* Launch contributor recognition framework
* Encourage onboarding assistance
* Support educational initiatives

Deliverables:

* Recognition criteria published
* Community participation tracking
* Educational campaigns initiated

---

## Phase 3 — Ecosystem Engagement

Objectives:

* Expand participation opportunities
* Foster collaboration across Hacash communities
* Encourage community-led initiatives

Deliverables:

* Community events
* Contributor spotlights
* Cross-community collaboration programs

---

## Phase 4 — Long-Term Sustainability

Objectives:

* Review governance processes
* Improve transparency mechanisms
* Maintain sustainable participation incentives

Deliverables:

* Periodic community reports
* Updated participation guidelines
* Ongoing community engagement initiatives

Note: This roadmap is indicative and may evolve based on community feedback, operational considerations, and ecosystem needs.
