# Frequently Asked Questions

## What is HCGD?

HCGD (Hacash Community Guardians) is a community recognition token created to acknowledge constructive contributions within the Hacash ecosystem.

## What is the purpose of HCGD?

The token is intended to support recognition programs related to:

* User education
* Community moderation
* Knowledge sharing
* Ecosystem participation

## What is the total supply?

The total supply is fixed at 1,000,000 HCGD.

## Is HCGD an investment product?

No.

HCGD is intended as a community participation and recognition token.

## Does holding HCGD provide ownership rights?

No.

Ownership of HCGD does not provide equity, revenue-sharing rights, or claims on project assets.

## Who may receive HCGD?

Community members who make meaningful contributions may be considered for recognition programs, subject to applicable guidelines.

## Can project plans change?

Yes.

Roadmaps, participation programs, and operational processes may evolve based on community needs and ecosystem developments.

## Where can users find official information?

Official project documentation and designated community channels should be considered the primary sources of information.
