## Intended Uses

HCGD may be utilized for:

* Recognition of community contributions
* Educational campaign participation
* Community engagement initiatives
* Volunteer appreciation programs
* Ecosystem activities

## Important Notice

HCGD is designed as a community participation and recognition token.

Ownership of HCGD does not grant:

* Equity ownership
* Revenue sharing rights
* Dividend rights
* Claims on project assets
* Guaranteed utility or future value
