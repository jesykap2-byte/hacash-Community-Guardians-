# Community Guidelines

The Hacash Community Guardians initiative promotes a respectful, collaborative, and educational environment.

## Expected Conduct

Participants are encouraged to:

* Treat others respectfully
* Assist newcomers
* Share accurate information
* Encourage productive discussions
* Support community growth

## Unacceptable Conduct

The following behaviors are prohibited:

* Harassment
* Hate speech
* Personal attacks
* Spam
* Fraudulent activity
* Impersonation
* Deliberate misinformation

## Contribution Recognition

Recognition may consider factors such as:

* Educational value
* Consistency
* Constructive participation
* Community impact
* Moderation support

## Transparency

Community decisions should be communicated clearly whenever practical.

## Enforcement

Moderation actions may be taken when participants violate these guidelines in order to maintain a healthy community environment.
