# Hacash Community Guardians (HCGD)

## Community Recognition for Ecosystem Contributors

Hacash Community Guardians (HCGD) is a community-focused fungible token created to recognize individuals who contribute positively to the Hacash ecosystem.

The initiative highlights the importance of:

* Helping new users learn about Hacash
* Providing educational resources
* Supporting community discussions
* Encouraging constructive participation
* Assisting moderation efforts

### Token Information

* Token: HCGD
* Type: Fungible Token (FT)
* Total Supply: 1,000,000
* Lots: 100
* Units per Lot: 10,000
* Stack Cost: 50 HAC

### Vision

To foster a welcoming, knowledgeable, and sustainable Hacash community through recognition of meaningful contributions.

### Disclaimer

HCGD is intended for community participation and recognition purposes. It does not represent ownership, equity, profit-sharing rights, or investment contracts.
